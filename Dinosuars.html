🦖 My 3 Favorite Dinosaurs 🦖

💙 Quetzalcoatlus 💙

This flying dino was the size of a PLANE, at an enormous 36ft long wings and would be one of the largest winged reptiles ever known to live on Earth. He stood at an average 16ft tall or higher. He fed on fish and other invertebrate by using his bat like wings to swoop down and snatch food with his beak like mouth with no teeth to swallow with one frightening gulp!!!
